About

Building clinically grounded AI with real-world constraints in mind.

I work at the intersection of machine learning, healthcare, and global health, designing robust models and decision-support systems that remain useful across populations, institutions, and data conditions.

My background spans signal processing, computational neuroscience, and applied ML, with projects ranging from epilepsy research to pediatric endocrinology and interoperable care workflows in Jamaica and the Galapagos Islands.

Research Themes

  • Generalization across populations and distribution shifts
  • Bias-aware evaluation of medical AI systems
  • Digital health infrastructure for low-resource settings

Collaborative Style

  • Practical deployment and workflow awareness
  • Transparent, reproducible experimentation
